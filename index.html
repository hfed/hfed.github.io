COMBED
Commercial Building Energy Dataset

Deployment

Our deployment spans across IIITD campus with over 200 smart meters recording various power related parameters every 30 s. The figure above shows our metering hierarchy.

About

Of all the sense points shown in the deployment above, COMBED contains a month of smart meter data collected from different sensing points in IIITD's academic building.

COMBED is the first energy related data set from a commercial building where data is sampled more than once a minute. Our data set comes with a loader allowing it to be easily plugged into nilmtk.

Our entire project entire analysis scripts is open source and accessible on Github.

Details about deployment

We use Schneider electric based smart meters to collect data every 30 seconds. Our data collection system consists of RaspberryPi based controller and sMAP for data visualization and archiving.
